About us

Welcome to the Learning Center

My English Academy provides an overview of the important trends such as ‘Communications’ play in business and day-to-day activities, especially in today’s dynamic situations.

We persistently strive to innovate finding new & more efficient ways to educate and serve students.

Our basic motto is to constantly improve & strengthen the positive impact we have on our students and society, helping them in transforming their lives through our dedication and hard-work. This encourages us to make a difference to the society.

My English Academy has been an iconic institute in delivering the best IELTS coaching in Ahmedabad . The institute is known for delivering a targeted and structured education according to the guidelines of the IELTS examination. My English Academy provides the candidates a complete package of IELTS preparation which is the amalgamation of the contemporary as well as International, thus leading to a better understanding by the students who want to study abroad. Furnished with professional teachers and trainers for most of the International Testing Systems for English, such as IELTS , PTE , TOEFL , and Spoken English. At My English Academy, we also conduct certificate workshops for aspirants who want to build their career as a trainer like, “Spoken English Train the Trainer” , “Life Skills Train the Trainer” and also Personality Development course for students, home makers and working professionals.
